Common Roles

What role could you play in a study group? Complete this survey to find out!

CO-ORDINATOR

Pulls people and tasks together. Good at co-ordinating resources.

SHAPER

Good at motivating and energizing others. urges team to meet goals.

PLANT

An ideas person; very creative and imaginative. Tends to be more concerned with the big picture.

MONITOR / EVALUATOR

Keeps team on track. Uses good logic and thinks things over.

RESOURCE INVESTIGATOR

Goes outside of the team to find information and ideas. Skilled at finding and using other resources.

IMPLENTER

Self-disciplined and practical. Breaks goals down into manageable tasks.

TEAM WORKER

Works well with different group members. Likeable, diplomatic, and supportive.

COMPLETER

A perfectionist who worries about details. Finishes tasks thoroughly.

The Planning Stage

  • Establish goals - what do people want to get out of the sessions? Who should be involved?
  • Determine how you would like to approach the material: Teach or review the material or come up with study questions
  • It can help to either assign someone the task of coordinating or get together to divide up the work
  • Ensure that everyone has something to do
  • Decide on a meeting time and place - think about where you work best and bring appropriate materials
